This Consent Notice explains the key things you are agreeing to when you use SuiteMeet. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

1. Age Confirmation

By using SuiteMeet, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age. SuiteMeet is not intended for children or minors.

2. Sharing Your Presence and Profile

When you turn on “Share my presence” and choose a visibility level (such as “Same hotel” or “Within radius”), you consent to:

  • Being shown to other SuiteMeet users within the visibility settings you choose.
  • Displaying the profile information you provide (such as display name and bio) to those users.

You can turn off sharing or change your visibility settings at any time from within the App.

3. Location-Based Features

SuiteMeet may request access to your device’s location to:

  • Show approximate distance to nearby hotels and demo users.
  • Help you find guests within your selected radius.

By granting location permission, you consent to the App using your location for these purposes. You can revoke this permission in your device settings.

4. Messaging and Interactions

When you send messages through SuiteMeet, you understand that:

  • Your messages are visible to the recipients you choose (e.g., hotel chat or direct messages).
  • You are responsible for the content you send and the interactions you choose to have with other users.

5. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Consent Notice from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Your continued use of the App after any changes means you agree to the updated Notice.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Consent Notice or how SuiteMeet uses your data and preferences, please contact the developer using the email address listed on the SuiteMeet app store listing.
