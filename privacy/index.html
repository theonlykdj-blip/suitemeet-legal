SuiteMeet Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how SuiteMeet (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the SuiteMeet mobile app.

1. What SuiteMeet Does

SuiteMeet is a hotel social app designed to help adult guests discover and connect with other guests who are staying at the same hotel or nearby hotels within a chosen radius.

2. Information We Collect

  • Profile information (local only) – Your display name, short bio, profile photo, and visibility preferences are stored on your device only using local storage. SuiteMeet does not operate a cloud account system.
  • Location (for Nearby only) – With your permission, the app uses your device’s approximate location to show demo hotel distances and nearby guests. Location data is used in-app and not stored on a remote server.
  • Presence & radius settings – Your choice to share presence and the miles radius you select are stored locally on your device.
  • Chat messages (local demo) – In this demo version, hotel and direct messages are stored locally on your device for your convenience. There is no remote messaging server.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Show you hotels and approximate distances within your selected radius
  • Let you control how visible your profile is to other guests
  • Display and manage your in-app chats with hotels and nearby guests

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Most data in SuiteMeet is stored locally on your device (profile, chats, preferences). If you delete the app or clear its data, that information is removed from your device.
  • We do not maintain a central database of your profile, chats, or location history.

5. Permissions

SuiteMeet may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Location – Used to calculate distances to nearby hotels and guests. Presence sharing is optional and can be turned off at any time.
  • Photos / Media – Used only if you choose to select a profile photo from your device.
  • Network access – Used for basic connectivity and future backend features (such as live hotel data) where available.

6. Children & Age Requirement

SuiteMeet is intended for adults age 18 and older. By using the app, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Third-Party Services

In its current demo form, SuiteMeet does not rely on external analytics or advertising SDKs. If future versions integrate third-party services, this policy will be updated to describe what is used and why.

8. Your Choices

  • You can toggle presence sharing on or off at any time in the Nearby screen.
  • You can change your profile visibility (e.g., same hotel only or within radius) from the Profile screen.
  • You may reset local profile data from the Settings screen, which clears your profile info stored on the device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of SuiteMeet after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the app store listing or the GitHub repository where this policy is hosted.
