SuiteMeet Consent Notice

Last updated: November 2025

By using the SuiteMeet mobile app, you are providing consent for the limited use of your information as described in this Consent Notice and our Privacy Policy.

1. Age Confirmation

By confirming that you are at least 18 years old in the in-app consent screen, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • You are legally an adult in your jurisdiction.
  • You understand that SuiteMeet is intended for adult hotel guests only.

2. Presence and Visibility

When you turn on presence sharing and adjust your visibility settings, you consent to:

  • Having your approximate presence (for example, “same hotel” or “within radius”) shown to other SuiteMeet users consistent with your settings.
  • Appearing in the “Who’s Here” or similar lists for users whose radius or hotel selection includes you.

3. Location-Based Features

When you grant the App permission to access your device’s location, you consent to the use of approximate location for:

  • Showing nearby hotels and approximate distances.
  • Determining whether you are “within radius” of other users.

You can withdraw this consent at any time by turning off location permissions in your device settings or by disabling presence sharing within the App.

4. Local Storage of Profile & Chat Data

You consent to the storage of your profile information (name, bio, visibility, and optional photo) and in-app messages on your device using local storage mechanisms. This data is primarily maintained on your device and can be cleared by:

  • Using the “Reset local profile data” option in Settings; and/or
  • Uninstalling the App or clearing its data via system settings.

5. Revoking Consent

You may revoke or modify parts of your consent at any time by:

  • Turning off presence sharing within the App.
  • Changing your profile visibility to “Private” or similar.
  • Adjusting or disabling location permissions in your device.
  • Clearing local profile data from Settings.
  • Uninstalling the App.

6. Relationship to the Privacy Policy and Terms

This Consent Notice works together with the SuiteMeet Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Using the App means you consent to the practices described in all three documents.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Consent Notice, you can contact the developer through the app store listing or the GitHub repository where this document is hosted.
